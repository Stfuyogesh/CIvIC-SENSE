Two Sides

The Harm vs. The Heart

🚫

When Empathy Is Missing

  • ✕ Tossing wrappers on the street — a sanitation worker picks it up in 40°C heat.
  • ✕ Vandalising park benches — families lose a place to rest and bond.
  • ✕ Honking endlessly — a patient in the nearby hospital can't sleep.
  • ✕ Spitting in stairwells — a neighbour feels disgusted in their own home.
  • ✕ Blocking footpaths with vehicles — a wheelchair user has no way through.
— VS —
💚

When Empathy Leads

  • ✓ Carrying your trash to a bin — the next person sees a clean pathway.
  • ✓ Offering your seat on a bus — an elderly stranger smiles for the first time today.
  • ✓ Keeping noise down after 10 PM — a baby next door sleeps peacefully.
  • ✓ Planting a tree in your lane — everyone breathes a little easier.
  • ✓ Reporting a broken street light — someone walks home safer tonight.
